Key development principles

Omni is a vertically integrated Ecosystem. Open at its core, Omni can power as many Frontends as you desire. Further systems and data sources can easily be connected. Thanks to the vertical integration approach and a host of best-of-breed solutions, most of the common destination challenges can be solved immediately.

Modular

The omni platform consists of modules that can be put together as required and supplemented over time.

This enables a very fast time-to-market for your solution and allows for staging the roll-out of new App functionality.

Additionally, our modular approach creates opportunites to 'go to market' often (versioning) and culminates with extensive digitization of the customer experience.

Flexible

Every destination is different. The Omni technology was therefore developed on a basis that allows the implementation of varying strategic focuses.

The ability of omni to segregate the presentation layer from the functional engine provides destinations and resorts with a solution that is on-brand, custom and closely aligned with achieving your wider business goals & KPIs.

Future Proofed

Open APIs and the advanced omni integration layer enable the integration of relevant systems' data sources- your existing data, third party (ie, ticketing) and entirely new feeds provided by inside labs.

By abstracting the data from the engine in this way, we ensure that the suite does not build up any legacy issues, remaining unhindered to use current, state-of-the-art technologies.
