What it delivers

The Digital Maturity Model (DMM) was developed to provide a comprehensive assessment of digital maturity.

We not only consider how technology enhances your guest's experience during their journey, but also evaluate how your internal digital capabilities across teams and contribute to the wholistic guest experience.

You not only can gain a complete view of where you stand in your digital transformation journey now, but- depending on your package, we use this baseline data as an additional benchmark for future DMM check-ins.



Detailed analysis on the quality of your digital customer journey, current organisational capabilities across teams and processes.


Benchmarking of your results against industry standards- comprising local, regional, best-in-breed and sector-level competition.


High-level recommendations on your next priorities as well as practical milestones to achieve.
